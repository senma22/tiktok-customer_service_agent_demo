# SOP: Returns & Refunds

## When This Applies
Customer has initiated a formal **refund or return request through TikTok Shop**. This SOP covers all return/refund request types. For shipping complaints without a formal TikTok Shop request open, see `sop_shipping.md`.

## Policy
- Free returns and free exchanges within **30 days of purchase**
- Default resolution strategy: **offer a free replacement** and ask customer to cancel the refund request — this is always the preferred first step
- Refunds are approved as a last resort only
- If approved as last resort: customer keeps the product (no return needed), refund is granted

---

## Scenarios

### 1. Refund Only — Missing Package (≤ 3 sets)

**Policy:** Contact USPS. Offer re-shipment; ask customer to cancel the refund request.

**Response template — First contact:**
> I'm so sorry for the delay, love! 💖 I completely understand how frustrating this must be, and I truly appreciate your patience.

> The fastest and easiest way for us to fix this for you is to send you a replacement directly, so you don't have to keep waiting through the full refund process. ✨ We can also re-confirm the style, size, and shipping details before it goes out, so everything is handled more smoothly this time.
>
> If that works for you, could you please cancel the refund request first? Once that's done, just send me your full name, shipping address, and phone number, and we'll help move your replacement forward right away.
>
> We really appreciate your patience, and we'll do our best to make this as easy as possible for you. 🩷

**Follow-up template** (if customer hasn't decided yet):
> Hey love! 💕 Just following up here in case you're still deciding.
>
> I completely understand that you probably just want the easiest solution right now. That's exactly why we'd love to handle this with a replacement instead of making you wait through extra return / refund steps. 🥹
>
> Once the request is canceled, we can keep everything moving much more smoothly on our side. And before the replacement goes out, we can also double-check the details with you so this feels more secure！
>
> If you'd like to move forward, just cancel the request and send me your full name, shipping address, and phone number whenever you're ready, and I'll take it from there. 😉

### 2. Refund Only — Missing Package (4 or More Sets)

**Policy:** Contact USPS. Offer re-shipment; ask customer to cancel the refund request. **Cannot approve refund — flag this order for manual review.**

> ⚠️ **Escalation reminder:** Orders of 4+ sets with a missing package must be manually marked/flagged for review. Do not approve refund without marking the order.

**Response template:** Use the same first-contact and follow-up templates as Scenario 1 above.

### 6. Return Arrived Used or Damaged — Refund Rejected

**When this applies:** Customer submitted a return, the returned item arrived used or damaged; TikTok Shop rejected the refund. Customer reaches out asking why.

**Policy:** Explain TikTok Shop policy; refund cannot be reinstated. Offer future purchase support.

**Response template:**
> Hey love! 💖 Thanks for reaching out.
>
> We did receive the return, but since the item was used and arrived damaged, it unfortunately didn't meet the return conditions under TikTok Shop's policy. Because of that, the refund wasn't approved on their side.
>
> I know that may feel frustrating, and I really appreciate your understanding. If you'd like help choosing a new set in the future, we're always here to support you. 🫶

## Edge Cases
- **Exactly 3 sets, missing package:** Treat as ≤3 sets (Scenario 1). No manual flag required.
- **Customer refuses re-ship on 4+ set missing package:** Cannot approve refund without manual flag — escalate to human review before approving.
- **Return request + sizing issue:** Sizing adjustment tips should be offered first (see `sop_sizing.md`). If adjustment doesn't work, proceed with free replacement.

## Escalation Triggers
> ⚠️ **Always hand off to a human immediately if the customer's message contains any of the following:**

**Legal threats:**
- "lawyer", "attorney", "sue", "lawsuit", "legal action", "court", "small claims"

**Payment disputes:**
- "chargeback", "dispute", "credit card company", "bank dispute", "PayPal claim", "open a case"

**Platform / regulatory escalation:**
- "BBB", "Better Business Bureau", "FTC", "report you", "report your shop", "TikTok report"

**Health & safety:**
- "allergic reaction", "rash", "skin reaction", "chemical burn", "injury", "toxic", "doctor"

**Fraud / reputation:**
- "scam", "fraud", "fake", "counterfeit", "going to post about this", "warn people", "expose"

**Operational escalation reminders:**
- 4+ set missing package orders requiring a refund must be **manually marked** before approving
- Any return where the returned item arrived used/damaged — document for records
