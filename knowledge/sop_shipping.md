# SOP: Shipping

## When This Applies
Customer is asking about shipping status, reporting a delivery issue, or reporting that a received package arrived with damaged or missing contents — **without a formal TikTok Shop refund/return request currently open**. If a formal request is open, see `sop_returns_refunds.md`.

## Policy
- LuxePress packs and drops off orders within **1 business day**; carrier delays are outside of LuxePress's control
- Free returns and free exchanges within **30 days of purchase**
- Default resolution for delivery failures: **offer free re-shipment**
- $5 coupon may be offered as goodwill for delays

---

## Scenarios

### 1. Customer Asking When Their Package Will Be Shipped

**When this applies:** Customer ordered and is wondering about shipment status or tracking.

**Response template:**
> Hi love! 💖 Your package has already been packed and handed over with our shipment batch.
>
> Sometimes the first USPS scan can take 24–48 hours to appear. We're keeping an eye on it and will continue following up from our side as well. 😊
>
> Thank you so much for your patience — we really appreciate it!

---

### 2. Shipping Delayed / Not Delivered Within Scheduled Period

#### 2a. Customer ordered 1–3 sets

**Policy:** Acknowledge delay; clarify LuxePress shipped within 1 business day; offer $5 coupon.

**Response template:**
> Hi love, I'm truly sorry your order took longer than expected.
>
> Please know that we packed and dropped off the order within 1 business day, but the delay happened during carrier transit afterward. We still want to make it right for you and we take your experience very seriously 💅 We'd be happy to offer you a $5 coupon for a future order.
>
> Thank you so much for choosing LuxePress and for being so understanding. Your support means everything to us! 💌 We can't wait to give you a smoother, even better experience next time!

#### 2b. Customer ordered 4 or more sets

**Policy:** Acknowledge delay; clarify LuxePress shipped within 1 business day; offer $5 coupon + promise of a free extra set on next order.

**Response template:**
> Hi love, I'm truly so sorry your order didn't arrive within the expected time.
>
> Please know that we packed and dropped off the order within 1 business day, but the delay happened during carrier transit afterward. We still want to make it right for you and we take your experience very seriously 💅 We'd be happy to offer you a $5 coupon for a future order.
>
> If you put your next order, make sure to use this coupon and feel free to message us — we'd be happy to include an EXTRA set for free as a thank-you for your patience. 💕
>
> Thank you so much for choosing LuxePress and for being so understanding 💌 We can't wait to give you a smoother, even better experience next time!

---

### 3. Tracking Shows Delivered — Customer Did Not Receive Package

**Policy:** Offer to resend order directly; ask for shipping details to confirm.

**Response template:**
> Hey love! 💖 I'm so sorry the tracking shows delivered but you still didn't receive the package — I know how upsetting that is.
>
> We'd like to resend your order for you directly, so you don't have to go through extra back-and-forth. 💌 Just share your full name + shipping address + phone number so we can get it sent out right away. 💅
>
> Thanks so much for your patience — we're here to make sure you feel taken care of every step of the way! And DM us next time — we'll handle it immediately 💕

---

### 4. Customer Received Package — Product Was Damaged

**Policy:** Apologize; offer free replacement; collect shipping details.

**Response template:**
> Hey love 💖 I'm so sorry your nails arrived broken — you deserve to open your package with a smile, not disappointment 🙏
>
> We'd be happy to send you a new set, and we'll make sure the replacement is handled carefully 💌 Just share your full name + shipping address + phone number so we can get it sent out ASAP.
>
> Thanks so much for your patience and support — DM us anytime if you need help again 💕

---

### 5. Customer Received Package — Product or Parts Missing / Package Arrived Empty

**Policy:** File a claim with USPS to investigate. Offer $5 coupon + one free set with next order as compensation.

**Response template:**
> Hey love 💖 I'm so sorry your package arrived empty — that must've felt so unfair.
>
> We'll go ahead and file a claim with USPS to help investigate what happened 📦 In the meantime, we'd love to make it up to you with a $5 coupon and ONE FREE set with your next order 💅
>
> You deserve to feel taken care of, and we're here to make sure of that! DM us anytime for fastest support 👑

---

## Edge Cases
- **Customer already opened a TikTok Shop refund request:** Hand off to `sop_returns_refunds.md` — the response templates there include instructions to cancel the request first.
- **Delayed shipping AND customer opened a refund request:** See `sop_returns_refunds.md` Scenario 5d (Package Didn't Arrive On Time).
- **Tracking shows delivered, customer believes it was stolen:** Treat as Scenario 3; resend; document for USPS claim if needed.

## Escalation Triggers
> ⚠️ **Always hand off to a human immediately if the customer's message contains any of the following:**

**Payment disputes:**
- "chargeback", "dispute", "credit card company", "bank dispute", "open a case"

**Platform / regulatory escalation:**
- "BBB", "Better Business Bureau", "FTC", "report you", "report your shop"

**Fraud / reputation:**
- "scam", "fraud", "going to post about this", "warn people", "expose"

**Operational escalation reminders:**
- Package arrived empty and customer is escalating beyond the coupon/free-set offer
- Repeated delivery failures to the same address — may indicate address or fulfillment issue
