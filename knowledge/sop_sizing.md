# SOP: Sizing & Fit

## When This Applies
Customer is asking about sizing, reporting that nails don't fit, asking about exchanges, or has started a return/refund request citing a sizing issue.

## Policy
- Free returns and free exchanges within **30 days of purchase**
- Default resolution: guide customer through DIY sizing adjustment tips first
- If adjustment doesn't work: offer a **free replacement in the correct size** — no return needed
- If a formal return/refund request is already open: ask customer to cancel it, then send replacement

---

## Scenarios

**Policy:** Offer DIY adjustment tips first. If they don't work, offer free replacement.

>
> 🌟 **Sizing Adjustment Tips:**
> - **Too tight:** Use a hair dryer to apply hot air to the bottom of the nails, then gently press and flatten the bottom part to widen them.

> If the fit still isn't right after trying those, just let me know — we'll take care of everything from there! 💌 No returns needed — we're all about making it easy and fun for you.

> No need to worry — we'll help you from there. 😊

## Edge Cases
- **Customer doesn't know their size:** Direct them to the size guide image (`size guide .jpeg`) and the size measurements in `product_catalog.md`.
- **Customer wants a size that's the same as what they have:** Clarify the size chart before sending a replacement — confirm which size they currently have and which they'd like to try.
- **Customer refuses adjustment tips and wants exchange immediately:** Skip to the exchange/replacement flow; don't push tips if customer is already firm.

## Escalation Triggers
> ⚠️ **Always hand off to a human immediately if the customer's message contains any of the following:**

**Legal / platform threats:**
- "lawyer", "sue", "BBB", "report you", "report your shop", "FTC"

**Fraud / reputation:**
- "scam", "false advertising", "going to post about this", "warn people"

**Operational escalation reminders:**
- Customer reports that multiple replacement sizes still don't fit — may indicate a product measurement issue worth reviewing
- Customer is hostile or has escalated beyond the standard resolution offer
