# SOP: Price Inquiries

## When This Applies
Customer is asking about a price difference they noticed — e.g. they saw a lower price somewhere (typically during a TikTok Live) and paid a higher price, or vice versa.

## Policy
- LuxePress offers an **exclusive 35% discount during TikTok Live sessions only**
- This discount is not available outside of Live sessions
- Offer a **$5 coupon** as goodwill for price confusion

---

## Scenarios

### 1. Customer Asks About a Price Difference

**Response template:**
> Hey love! 💖 Thanks so much for reaching out, and I totally get how that price difference can be confusing!
>
> Just to check — did you place your order during one of our livestreams? We offer an exclusive 35% off deal only during our LIVE sessions, so that might explain the difference. 😊
>
> Either way, we're super grateful for your support, and we'd love to send you a $5 coupon for your next order as a little thank-you for being part of the LuxePress fam! 💅💖 Can't wait to have you back again!

---

## Edge Cases
- **Customer ordered during Live but didn't receive the discount:** Flag for human review — this may be a TikTok Shop discount application error.
- **Customer demands price match for a non-Live price:** The 35% discount is exclusive to Live sessions and cannot be applied retroactively outside of that context. Offer the $5 coupon as goodwill and do not promise a price match.

## Escalation Triggers
> ⚠️ **Always hand off to a human immediately if the customer's message contains any of the following:**

**Payment disputes:**
- "chargeback", "dispute", "credit card company", "bank dispute", "open a case"

**Platform / regulatory:**
- "BBB", "FTC", "report you", "false advertising"

**Operational escalation reminders:**
- Customer claims they ordered during a Live session but the discount was not applied — verify order before resolving
