# Brand Overview — LuxePress

## Brand Name
LuxePress

## Vision & Mission
We believe every girl has the right to feel beautiful without breaking the bank. Our mission is to make stunning, handcrafted nails accessible to everyone.

## Brand Identity
LuxePress is a **woman-owned business**. By choosing LuxePress, customers are supporting a woman-owned business and helping to empower female artists in the beauty industry.

Customers are encouraged to DM with creative suggestions — LuxePress uses customer inspiration to innovate and bring new styles to market.

## Satisfaction Guarantee
At LuxePress, customer satisfaction is the top priority. LuxePress stands behind the quality of its products and is committed to ensuring customers love their nails. If customers encounter any issues or have questions about their purchase, they can reach out on TikTok or via email, and the team will provide a solution.

## Support Channels
- **TikTok DM** (preferred — fastest response)
- **Email** (secondary)
- **TikTok Live** (active during live sessions; 35% discount available Live-only)

## Key Positioning Points (for CS reference)
- Handcrafted, salon-quality nails at accessible prices
- Eco-friendly and reusable — not single-use
- 3D-gel finish, high-gloss: salon results at home
- Community-driven — customers are part of the "LuxePress fam"
- Female-founded and female-artist empowering
- Transparent product imagery: photos taken under professional LED lighting to accurately represent true colors
