### Sizing Adjustment Tips (CS reference — for sizing inquiries)
- **Too tight:** Use a hair dryer to apply hot air to the bottom of the nails, then gently press and flatten the bottom part to widen them.
- **Too loose:** Use the provided nail file to carefully file the edges until the nails fit perfectly.

If adjustments don't resolve the fit issue, offer a free replacement in the correct size. No return needed.
